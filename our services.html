Our Services

Our services are provided to make sure that artists are pushed to the point to where their career is in place. Our services that we give are helping artists, giving advice to artists, and more. We provide the money for the recordings and the sessions. The recordings are $30.00 a recording and sessions are $20.00 each sessions. We provide the money to help our artists and make sure that they have no way to destroy their career. We also take artists out to meet and see other artists so that they can get a view of how they will be coming in to the industry. When we take artists to see other artists we provide a nice Mercedes Benz that we will have Mr. Tishone taking us around in. We provide food, drinks, we even take the artists shopping with our money that we provide. Also artists are allowed to buy or pick up anything that they want. We have the right amount of money to make sure that all of the products are paid for. Another service we provide is taking artists on live tours and shows to give them the feeling on how they will feel when they start performing. We include CD's of the artists so that their music is put out and heard by other artists and people. When their music is out and on the role we take the artists to meet on fan that they really have an interest in this can include anyone.
